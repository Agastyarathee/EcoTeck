Imagine a car that could provide you with real-time information about your surroundings, traffic conditions, and upcoming hazards. This information could be displayed on a heads-up display (HUD) that is projected onto the windshield in front of you. The HUD could also be used to display directions, traffic updates, and other information. With minimal and important information it wont effect your view or driving experience. It will come with features like one for learners which helps the vehicle and front and at back to maintain safe distance.
